why i fly to the sky?


Cz the sky is always vast and limitless, offering a freedom that cannot be found on the ground. I fly to the sky to chase my dreams, to leave behind the boundaries that hold me back, and to feel the peace that comes with soaring high. Among the clouds, all worries seem small, and it's just me and the wind carrying my hopes. The sky is a place where dreams feel closer, where I can see the world from a different perspective. So, I fly to the sky not just to escape, but to discover the true meaning of freedom and dreams.
